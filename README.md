# Pedro 🧠🚪

### Because your self-control isn't enough.

Look, generic app blockers are boring. You either ignore them or you just disable the timer. **Pedro** is different. He doesn't just block your apps; he makes you earn your distractions. It's tough love for your digital addiction.

## 🕹️ How it works

It's actually very simple:
1.  **Stop the urge**: You try to open Instagram/TikTok/X.
2.  **The Checkpoint**: Pedro intercepts you. You have two choices:
    *   **Get back to work**: Close the app and go do something productive (Pedro tracks your discipline).
    *   **The Challenge**: Solve **5 logic or math questions** in a row.
3.  **Don't mess up**: If you get a question wrong, you're hit with a **2-minute mandatory cooldown**. No excuses.
4.  **Leaving is harder**: Want to stop monitoring an app? You'll have to pass a **10-question master quiz**. Pedro doesn't let you quit that easily.

## ✨ Features

*   **⏱ Focus Mode (Pomodoro)**: Need to study? One tap blocks everything for 25+ minutes. No quizzes, no bypass. Just focus.
*   **📈 Weekly Insights**: Pedro compares your usage to last week and gives you the hard truth ("30% more than last week").
*   **📊 Beautiful Charts**: Track your weekly social media usage with custom-built, minimalist charts directly on the home screen.
*   **⏰ Per-App Limits**: Set independent daily limits for each app (e.g., 30m for Instagram, 15m for TikTok). Once reached, the app is hard-blocked.
*   **📅 Scheduled Blocks**: Block apps entirely during specific hours (e.g., 22:00 - 07:00).
*   **🧠 Progressive Difficulty**: Answer too many questions correctly? Pedro automatically makes the questions harder. Fail too many? He might go easy on you.
*   **💬 Motivational Quotes**: Interstitial screens feature tough-love quotes to make you reconsider opening that app.
*   **🌙 Evening Summary**: Get a daily push notification at 21:00 summarizing your discipline (or lack thereof).
*   **📱 Home Screen Widget**: Minimalist widget showing your daily social usage and active Focus Mode timer.
*   **📤 Share Stats**: Flex your discipline by sharing your daily stats with one tap.

## ⚙️ Technical Details

*   **Zero Placeholders**: 500+ unique questions (Language, Logic, Math).
*   **Deep Integration**: Accessibility Service makes the block real.
*   **Sleek looks**: A premium, brutalist dark UI. Focus doesn't have to be ugly.
*   **Multilingual**: Native support for IT, EN, ES, and DE.

## 🛠️ Download & Run

1.  Download and run the APK.
2.  **Crucial**: You MUST grant *Usage Access*, *Post Notifications*, and *Accessibility* permissions in settings for Pedro to do his job.
